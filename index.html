About Me

I am a board-certified Internal Medicine physician with a passion for delivering comprehensive, patient-centered care. My medical journey began at Ewha Womans Medical College in South Korea, followed by rigorous residency training at Rochester General Hospital in New York.

Throughout my career, I have served in a variety of rural and community settings, including Newark Wayne County in upstate New York, as well as Culpeper and Madison County in Virginia. I am known for my thorough and attentive approach, especially in helping patients who have not been in care for some time. I take particular pride in reconnecting these individuals with essential preventive services—such as cancer and hepatitis C screening—and have helped many patients regain control of their health through these important interventions.
